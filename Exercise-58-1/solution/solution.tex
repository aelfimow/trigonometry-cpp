\section{Exercise 58.1}

$f(x) = \sin{x} \cot{x}$\\
\\
$\cot{x} = \frac{\cos{x}}{\sin{x}} \Rightarrow f(x) = \sin{x} \frac{\cos{x}}{\sin{x}} = \cos{x}$
\\
\\
Solution:\\
$\sin{x} \cot{x} = \cos{x}$
